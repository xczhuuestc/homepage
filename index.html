    Research

    My general research interest is using computational learning theory and information theory to address how agents use state abstraction and action abstraction in reinforcement learning to perceive the world and reason. I also work on enabling innovative machine learning and reinforcement learning algorithms to tackle real-world challenges with broad societal implications, such as quantum computing and fundamental bioinformatics problems.
