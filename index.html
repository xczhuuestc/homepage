Xianchao Zhu

I am a Lecturer at the School of Artificial Intelligence and Big Data, at Henan University of Technology. I am also a postdoc working with Lei Cai at Henan University of Technology. Before that, I completed my Ph.D. from Institute of Fundamental and Frontier Sciences (IFFS), University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2022, where I research machine learning, reinforcement learning and quantum reinforcement learning. I'm fortunate to be co-advised by William Zhu and Xiaoting Wang. Previously, I was working with Xianjun Shen and Xingpeng Jiang on machine learning and bioinformatics at Central China Normal University and received my M.S. degree in 2018.

    Research

    My general research interest is using computational learning theory and information theory to address how agents use state and action abstraction in reinforcement learning to perceive the world and reason. I also work on enabling innovative machine learning and reinforcement learning algorithms to tackle real-world challenges with broad societal implications, such as quantum computing and fundamental bioinformatics problems.
